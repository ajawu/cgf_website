About Us

We connect, impact and grow together

Change does not happen unless you make it happen. The Classic Gentlemen Foundation was founded based on bringing about change in our environment. We do this by networking, creating businesses and directly impacting our immediate environment.

Our Mission

To Support and Empower one another, to succeed with family and career, to continually improve on our socialization skills for the relaxation of all members and to inspire a healthier community by supporting the needy and the less privileged (CSR).

Our ViSION STATEMENT

A group of gentlemen with the aim of socializing, impacting positively on our immediate environment and creating businesses.
